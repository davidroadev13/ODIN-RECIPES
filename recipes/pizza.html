Italian Pizza Recipe

Hello friends, today I’m going to show you how to make an authentic Italian pizza at home. You’ll see that with just a few simple ingredients and a regular oven, you can make a pizza just as good, or almost as good, as the one you’d get in a great trattoria.

Ingredients

  • 680g strength flour
  • 485g water
  • 9g fresg yeast
  • 15g salt
  • 30g sugar
  • 25g extra virgin olive oil

Step By Step Preparation

  1. Put the crumbled yeast in a large bowl
  2. Add the water of about 25°C
  3. Add the sugar and yeast are diluted
  4. add the oil and stir
  5. Sift the flour
  6. Add the salt. Stir until you stop seeing the flour particles
  7. Close the container and let the dough rest for 20 minutes
  8. After a while, flod the dough again in the form of an envelope. We fold the dough into an envelope to develop the gluten
  9. Leave the most at rest for another 20 minutes
  10. Fold the dough into an envelope again
  11. Take 3 or 4 plastic bags and spread them inside with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil
  12. Weigh the dough and divide in into 3 or 4 pieces, to make 3 large o 4 medium pizzas
  13. The pizza dough is ready to be baked
  14. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  15. Bake the pizza for 15 minutes
  16. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool down for 10 minutes
  17. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool down for 10 minutes
  18. Cut the pizza into slices
